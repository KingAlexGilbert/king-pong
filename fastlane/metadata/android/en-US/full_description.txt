King Pong is a retro arcade Pong game with an 18-level campaign, custom games, local and LAN multiplayer, progressive paddle shapes, save slots, multiple languages, touch controls, controller support, and a secret ending.

Features:
* 18 campaign levels across three leagues
* Custom games and local two-player mode
* Optional LAN multiplayer
* Progressive paddle shapes and changing arenas
* Touch, keyboard, and controller support
* Multiple languages and three local save slots
* Original programmatically generated music and sound
* Secret boss battle and ending

King Pong works offline and contains no advertising, analytics, accounts, tracking, or in-app purchases.
